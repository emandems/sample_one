Really Get to Know Me

Welcome to my Page. If you want to understand me...

simply imagine a river. It continuously expels movement, sometimes rapidly and other times with a calm flow. Each drop of water is always moving, always changing. Now look at it from an arial view. It twists and turns randomly for seemingly no reason, but in actuality, the different rock formations it passes or eleveation it crosses are what determine the river's route. That's my life- I'm constantly moving and changing, whether geographically or in my mind. From an arial view, my chocies look random and unreasonble, but take a moment and have a closer look, simply ask, and my life is mostly a well-reasoned, collision of random events that had a purpose.

I graduated with a B.A. in English because I like to read and write.

I graduated with a M.A. in Teaching because I wanted to travel the world and teach English.

Continuing back to my roots- I began freelance writing for several Memphis publications while supporting myself financially being a barista/shift leader at Otherlands Coffee Bar. I also got introduced to SEO, content writing, html and Google Analytics.

I applied for a M.A. in Journalism degree taking place in 2 European universities in a joint-degree program, was accepted and studied alongside professional and aspiring journalists from 32 different nationalities.

Living abroad + writing + computer science= me.
